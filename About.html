About Us

Paz Productions is a St. Lucian based digital media organization founded in May 2020. Paz Productions was formed and established by Deandre Martyr, a talented St. Lucian-born digital media artiste. Deandre is currently pursuing an Associate's Degree in Digital Media. At Paz Productions, we offer a variety of services including photography, video editing, videography, graphics design and logo designs. We provide consultation services, conceptualization, development and creation of art pieces. We bring the ideas of our clients to life. At Paz Productions, our customers are our number one priority. Therefore, we communicate with our clients throughout the entire creative process; from conceptualization of the design/idea to the final product. At Paz Productions we strive to achieve the perfect blend of professionalism and creativity. Contact us for all your creative needs!
